==================================================
SMOOTHNESS & MOTION DESIGN UPDATE
==================================================

VERY IMPORTANT:
The website must feel extremely smooth, fluid, immersive, and organic.

The experience should feel:
- elegant
- cinematic
- flowing
- soft
- premium
- calming
- alive
- modern
- tactile

The scrolling experience should feel:
like floating through a tropical Kerala retreat.

NOT:
- rigid
- static
- abrupt
- mechanical
- boxy

==================================================
OVERALL MOTION PHILOSOPHY
==================================================

Animations should feel:
- natural
- breathable
- gentle
- organic
- luxurious
- emotionally immersive

The website should feel:
alive with motion.

Every interaction should feel:
intentional and smooth.

Think:
Apple-level smoothness +
boutique hospitality elegance +
tropical calmness.

==================================================
SCROLL EXPERIENCE
==================================================

Implement:
- buttery smooth scrolling
- inertia scrolling
- smooth section transitions
- floating layered motion
- organic reveal animations

Use:
Lenis smooth scrolling
OR
GSAP ScrollSmoother
OR
Framer Motion smooth scroll techniques.

The page should glide smoothly.

==================================================
SECTION TRANSITIONS
==================================================

Sections should NOT abruptly appear.

Each section should:
- softly fade in
- slide subtly upward
- reveal gradually
- feel layered and immersive

Use:
- staggered animation timing
- opacity transitions
- smooth transforms
- depth-based movement

==================================================
IMAGE ANIMATIONS
==================================================

Images should feel cinematic and alive.

Use:
- slow hover zoom
- parallax image movement
- soft scale transitions
- layered image depth
- floating tropical movement

Gallery images should:
- slightly elevate on hover
- zoom smoothly
- animate with soft easing

Avoid:
- harsh scaling
- aggressive motion
- fast transitions
